FSC Medical Programs

FSC Medical Lab Technician

The Medical Lab Technician program prepares students to conduct medical tests and operate lab equipment, assisting in patient diagnosis and treatment. The program covers topics such as microbiology, hematology, and clinical chemistry.

FSC Dispenser Technology

This program teaches students how to prepare and dispense medications, handle prescriptions, and assist in patient counseling. It includes topics like pharmacology, pharmacy law, and drug interactions.

FSC Cardiology Technology

This program focuses on the technology behind cardiology. Students learn how to operate ECG machines, monitor heart health, assist with diagnostic procedures, and handle cardiology-related equipment.

FSC Operation Theater Technology

This program trains students to assist in surgical operations, manage sterile equipment, prepare the operating room, and support surgical teams during medical procedures.

FSC Radiology Technology

The Radiology Technology program teaches students to perform imaging procedures, such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs. Students learn how to prepare patients, operate imaging machines, and ensure accurate results for diagnosis.

FSC Physiotherapy Technology

This program focuses on rehabilitation techniques for patients with physical injuries or illnesses. Students learn to assist in the treatment of musculoskeletal disorders, manage physiotherapy equipment, and guide patients in recovery exercises.

FSC Dental Technology

This program teaches students to design and fabricate dental prosthetics such as crowns, bridges, dentures, and implants. They learn about dental materials, dental anatomy, and dental laboratory procedures.

FSC Ophthalmic Technology

This program covers eye care, including learning to perform eye exams, operating ophthalmic equipment, and assisting with diagnosing and treating eye diseases. Students are trained to support ophthalmologists in clinical settings.

Diploma Programs

Diploma Dispenser

The Diploma in Dispenser program offers training in pharmaceutical practices, including the dispensing of medications, maintaining accurate patient records, and understanding drug interactions and contraindications.

Diploma Operation Theater

This program teaches students to assist during surgeries, manage surgical instruments, and maintain hygiene and sterility in the operating room. The program also covers patient care before and after surgical procedures.

BS Allied Health Sciences (4 Years)

BS Clinical Sciences

This 4-year program provides students with in-depth knowledge in clinical research, medical diagnostics, and the management of healthcare systems. Graduates are equipped to work in clinical laboratories, research institutes, and healthcare facilities.

BS Emergency

This program focuses on emergency care and trauma management. Students learn advanced first aid, emergency response strategies, and critical care techniques to assist in life-threatening situations and disaster management.

Can I shift to medical if I passed my matric in computer science? +
Yes, you can shift to medical. Our programs are open to students from diverse educational backgrounds, including those who completed matric in computer science.
By which board do we have affiliation? +
Our programs are affiliated with Punjab Medical Faculty Lahore, Mirpur, and the Federal Board of Education.
Is there an age limit for applying to medical programs? +
There is no strict age limit for applying. However, students are required to meet the minimum academic qualifications to be eligible for admission.
Do you offer part-time programs for working professionals? +
Yes, we offer part-time programs for certain medical fields to accommodate working professionals who wish to enhance their skills.
Are your medical programs recognized internationally? +
Yes, our medical programs are recognized by international medical boards and universities, ensuring global career opportunities for our graduates.
